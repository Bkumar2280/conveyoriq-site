NCR-2024-0038 · CRITICAL · IN REMEDIATION
Anchor bolt torque below minimum on drive frame fixings
AI finding
Drive frame anchor bolts recorded at 28–31 Nm across four fixings. Specification requires 45 Nm ± 5%. Observed values are 31–38% below minimum. Risk of frame movement under full operational load and vibration.
AI remediation plan
1
Isolate and lock out the conveyor per site LOTO procedure before commencing work
2
Inspect all four drive frame anchor bolt locations for signs of movement or elongation
3
Re-torque all anchor bolts to 45 Nm using a calibrated torque wrench — verify with a second pass
4
Photograph each bolt with torque wrench reading visible for photographic evidence record
5
Upload closure photos to this NCR and mark as resolved for final sign-off
